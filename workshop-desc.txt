[h1]Function[/h1]
[b]By using this mod you can make [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=2993766165]VNR [/url] and [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3413890094]Ultimate Tech Tree: New Horizon[/url] Compatible.[/b]

[b]Unlike most compatible mods, this mod allows you to expand your VNR into the 90s![/b]

The naval design of UTTNH follows the vanilla naval designer, and the AI cannot design ships, which greatly reduces the playability of naval battles in the middle and late stages of the game. VNR solves these two problems, but the naval technology tree of VNR only reaches 1955, and the two are incompatible with each other.

By using this mod, VNR can be made compatible with UTTNH. At the same time, VNR's naval designer and AI naval design are used, and the technology is guided to the 1990s.
